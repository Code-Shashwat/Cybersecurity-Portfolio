# Incident Report: Snapped Phishing Line

Platform : Try Hack me

## Scenario
Multiple employs received a phishing email attempting credential harvesting.

## Investigation
- Analyzed email content and sender details
- Checked website and it's content
- Verified Document authenticity with theat intelligence tools.

## Findings 
- Domain mismatch  
- Social engineering techniques used
- Malicious document found on website(Trojan)

## Indicators Identified
- Phishing URL  
- Suspicious domain
- Malicious Document

## Conclusion
Credential harvesting phishing attempt.

## Recommendation
- Block domain  
- Enable MFA
- Change comparamised credentials.
- Conduct user awareness training
