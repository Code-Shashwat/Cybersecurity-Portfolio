# Incident Report: Greenholt Phish

Platform : Try hack me 

## Scenario
The Suspicious email received from customer with a generic greeting, an unexpected request for a money transfer, and an unsolicited attachment.

## Investigation
- Analyzed email content and sender details  
- Verified authenticity of attached document with virus total
- Verified email headers for spoofing indicators  (SPF abd DMARC

## Findings
- Malicious attachment  
- Indicators consistent with phishing attempt  

## Indicators Identified
- Malicious document (Ransomeware)


## Conclusion
Confirmed phishing attempt targeting user.

## Recommendation
- Implement email filtering rules
